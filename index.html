What's New

  • Farewell 2021

    As time goes by, students of Grade XII of Session 2020-21 say goodbye to there school for the future in a fun way. The younger grades performed many things to impress the Batch of 2020-21. The farewell was fun and it was on Zoom. Principal Swaraj was the special guest in this. This is the first Online THSVK Farewell.

  • Republic Day Celebrations

    During online classes, the school celebrated the 72nd Republic Day virtually with all students. This event showed pride and freeedom to all heritans showing how proud is to be an indian. There were a lot of patriotic performences along with the flag hoisting. The velocity of independence got risen as everyone gets inspired by our Chief Guest-Wing Commander Barnali Mitra. This was the 1st virtual Republic Day Celebration and the 2nd National Day Event. The first was of independence Day 2020 on Google Meet while this was on Zoom.
